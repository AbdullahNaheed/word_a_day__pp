Privacy Policy

Word-A-Day Language Builder does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

This app may display ads provided by third-party networks such as TopOn. These ad services may collect limited non-personal data like device type, ad interactions, and approximate location to show relevant ads. We do not control or store this data.

Data Storage

All progress and user data (such as word history, quiz results, and streaks) are stored locally on your device. No cloud or external servers are used.

Children's Privacy

Word-A-Day Language Builder does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.
